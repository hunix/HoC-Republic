## Draft positioning

HoC-Republic should be positioned as an **open-source AI-agent civilization lab** rather than as a finished commercial product. The hook can mention AI citizens, digital-genome inheritance, family lineages, governance, and child-agent creation, but the copy must say that these are implemented as digital simulation and orchestration mechanics under human-defined constraints.

## Non-spam rules

Do not ask for upvotes, stars, artificial engagement, or coordinated voting. Ask readers to inspect the source, run the quick start, file issues, propose demos, or leave technical feedback.
